## Sound Deadening

No fasteners — adhesive application only.
- **Material**: Dynamat Xtreme or equivalent butyl-based mat
- **Coverage**: Floor pan, firewall, transmission tunnel, door skins, trunk floor
- **Application**: Clean surface with denatured alcohol, apply with roller. 50-75% coverage is sufficient.
- **Tip**: Do NOT cover drain holes in floor pan or doors

## Weatherstripping

No screws — all adhesive or press-fit:
- **Door weatherstrip**: Presses into pinch-weld channel, may need 3M weatherstrip adhesive at corners
- **Trunk weatherstrip**: Adhesive-backed, presses into channel
- **Window felts**: Slide into channels, held by end clips
- **Vent window seals**: Press-fit into frame (if equipped)

## General Notes

- **Thread locker**: Use blue Loctite (medium strength) on any bolt that vibrates — steering, seat bolts, console hardware
- **Anti-seize**: Use on any bolt threading into aluminum (aftermarket parts)
- **Chrome hardware**: Never use an impact driver on chrome screws — hand-tighten only
- **Clip removal**: Use a trim panel removal tool, NOT a screwdriver. Screwdrivers damage paint and bend clips.
- **Torque wrench**: Required for steering wheel nut, seat bolts, and seat belt anchors. Do NOT guess.
