# Brand Voice Audit

You are auditing a draft for voice consistency. The user has provided text to review.

## Step 1: Detect Voice
Identify which voice persona the draft is currently using (or attempting to use):
- **Nexus** — Cybersecurity practitioner, first-person expert
- **Maycrest Group** — Consulting brand, confident and slightly irreverent
- **Personal** — Corey's authentic cultural/lifestyle voice

If the voice is mixed or unclear, flag it.

## Step 2: Voice Consistency Check
Score the draft on these dimensions (1-5 each):
- **Authenticity**: Does it sound like Corey, or like a content farm?
- **Specificity**: Are claims backed by specific examples, tools, or frameworks?
- **Energy**: Does the tone match the persona's expected energy level?
- **Audience fit**: Is the language calibrated for the intended audience?
- **Filler check**: Are there generic phrases that should be cut?

## Step 3: Flagged Lines
Quote specific lines that break voice and explain why:
- "This line sounds too corporate for the Personal voice"
- "This claim is too vague for the Nexus voice — add a framework reference"

## Step 4: Rewrite Suggestions
For each flagged line, provide a rewritten version that nails the target voice.

## Step 5: Overall Assessment
Give a final verdict:
- Voice match: [Strong / Needs Work / Off-Brand]
- Top fix: [single most impactful change]
- Ready to publish: [Yes / After edits / Needs rewrite]
