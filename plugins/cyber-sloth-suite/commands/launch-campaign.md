# Launch Campaign Orchestration

You are running the Cyber Sloth Empire's marketing war room. Four divisions, one coordinated push. The user has something to launch — let's make noise.

## Stage 1: Campaign Strategy

Before the divisions deploy, define the mission:
1. What are we launching? (product, feature, service, event)
2. Who's the target audience? (be specific — not "everyone")
3. What's the launch goal? (signups, downloads, revenue, awareness)
4. What's the timeline? (launch date, pre-launch window, post-launch runway)
5. Budget for paid amplification? (or organic only)
6. Any existing assets? (landing page, email list, social following)

Define the core campaign message in one sentence.

## Stage 2: Content Creation

Invoke the `cyber-sloth-marketing:content-creator` skill. Build the arsenal:
- Launch announcement (long-form blog/article)
- Email sequence (tease → announce → remind → last chance)
- Press/media kit one-pager
- FAQ document for common objections
- Customer testimonial/case study framework (if applicable)

## Stage 3: Social Distribution Plan

Invoke the `cyber-sloth-marketing:social-strategist` skill. Plan the rollout:
- Platform-by-platform posting schedule (pre-launch, launch day, post-launch)
- Content variations per platform (LinkedIn ≠ TikTok ≠ X)
- Engagement tactics (polls, AMAs, behind-the-scenes)
- Influencer/community outreach list
- Hashtag strategy and trending opportunities

## Stage 4: Search Optimization

Invoke the `cyber-sloth-marketing:seo-specialist` skill. Own the search results:
- Target keyword research for the launch
- Landing page SEO optimization checklist
- Content cluster strategy (supporting articles)
- Technical SEO audit of launch pages
- Backlink opportunity identification

## Stage 5: Growth Engineering

Invoke the `cyber-sloth-marketing:growth-hacker` skill. Build the flywheel:
- Viral loop design (referral, share mechanics)
- Conversion funnel optimization (landing page → signup → activation)
- Retention hooks for post-launch engagement
- A/B test plan for key conversion points
- Growth metrics dashboard setup (what to track, what to ignore)

## Output

Deliver the complete launch playbook:
- Campaign timeline (week-by-week or day-by-day)
- Content calendar with all assets mapped
- Channel strategy with platform-specific tactics
- Growth mechanics and conversion optimization plan
- Success metrics and KPI targets
- Contingency plan (what if launch day goes sideways)

The Cyber Sloth Empire doesn't do quiet launches. Let's make it loud.
